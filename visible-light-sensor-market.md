<p><ul><li>Flash Effect Sensor</li><li>External Photoelectric Effect Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Visible Light Sensor market encompasses various types, including Flash Effect Sensors and External Photoelectric Effect Sensors. Flash Effect Sensors primarily detect brief bursts of light, commonly used in photography and mobile devices to optimize exposure. In contrast, External Photoelectric Effect Sensors operate by measuring light intensity and are utilized in automation, security systems, and environmental monitoring. Both sensor types enhance functionality in applications that depend on accurate light detection, improving user experience and system efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
