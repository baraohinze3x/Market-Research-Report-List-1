<p><p>The Flip-Chip Package Substrate market encompasses various types, primarily focused on FCBGA (Flip-Chip Ball Grid Array) and FCCSP (Flip-Chip Chip Scale Package). FCBGA substrates are characterized by their ability to house large die and provide excellent electrical performance, making them ideal for high-density applications in computing and telecommunications. FCCSP, on the other hand, offers a compact design with minimal footprint, suitable for consumer electronics and mobile devices. Both types enable efficient thermal management and improved signal integrity in advanced semiconductor packaging.</p></p>
