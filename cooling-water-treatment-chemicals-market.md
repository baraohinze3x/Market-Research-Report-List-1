<p><p>The cooling water treatment chemicals market encompasses several key types, including scale inhibitors, corrosion inhibitors, and biocides. Scale inhibitors prevent the formation of mineral deposits that can obstruct pipes and heat exchangers. Corrosion inhibitors protect metal surfaces from deterioration caused by water and contaminants, extending equipment lifespan. Biocides are used to control the growth of harmful microorganisms, ensuring the efficiency and safety of cooling systems. Together, these chemicals enhance operational efficiency and reduce maintenance costs in industrial water cooling processes.</p></p>
