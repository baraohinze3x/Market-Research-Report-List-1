<p><ul><li>Dehydrated Dried Soup</li><li>Instant Dried Soup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dried soup market consists of two main types: dehydrated dried soup and instant dried soup. Dehydrated dried soup is made by removing moisture from soup ingredients, requiring rehydration before consumption. This type often contains more natural flavors and ingredients. In contrast, instant dried soup is pre-cooked and then dried, allowing for rapid preparation by simply adding hot water. Both types cater to consumers seeking convenience, portability, and long shelf life, making them popular choices for quick meals.</p></p>
