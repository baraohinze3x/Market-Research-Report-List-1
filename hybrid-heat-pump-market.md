<p><p>The hybrid heat pump market primarily consists of two types: compression heat pumps and absorption heat pumps. Compression heat pumps utilize electrical energy to compress refrigerant, generating heat efficiently for residential and commercial applications. In contrast, absorption heat pumps leverage heat sources, like natural gas or solar energy, to drive the heating process, making them suitable for larger installations. Both types complement each other by providing energy-efficient heating solutions, contributing to reduced carbon footprints and increasing energy conservation across various sectors.</p></p>
