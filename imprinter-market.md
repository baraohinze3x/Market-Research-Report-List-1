<p><strong>The Imprinter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relief Printing Press</li><li>Planographic Press</li><li>Intaglio Printing Press</li><li>Screen-process Printing Press</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The imprinter market is categorized into four main types: relief printing presses, planographic presses, intaglio printing presses, and screen-process printing presses. Relief printing uses raised surfaces for ink application, ideal for mass production of images and text. Planographic printing, including offset techniques, relies on flat surfaces where ink and water repulse each other. Intaglio printing involves incised images where ink fills the recesses, producing high-quality prints. Lastly, screen-process printing uses mesh screens to transfer ink onto various substrates, favored for vibrant colors and versatility.</p></p>
