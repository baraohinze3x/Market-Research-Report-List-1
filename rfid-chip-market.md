<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RFID Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low frequency (LF) RFID Chip</li><li>High frequency (HF) RFID Chip</li><li>Ultrahigh frequency (UHF) RFID Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID chip market is categorized into three main types based on frequency. Low Frequency (LF) RFID chips operate at 125-134 kHz, offering short read ranges and robust performance in challenging environments, ideal for animal tracking and access control. High Frequency (HF) RFID chips function at 13.56 MHz, suitable for applications like smart cards and inventory management, with moderate read distances. Ultrahigh Frequency (UHF) RFID chips, operating from 860 to 960 MHz, provide longer ranges and faster read speeds, making them ideal for supply chain and logistics applications.</p></p>
