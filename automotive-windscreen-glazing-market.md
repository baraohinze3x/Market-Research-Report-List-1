<p><ul><li>Laminated Glass</li><li>Tempered Glass</li><li>Polycarbonate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive windscreen glazing market comprises three main types: laminated glass, tempered glass, and polycarbonate. Laminated glass consists of two glass layers with a plastic interlayer, offering safety and reduced noise. Tempered glass is heat-treated for strength and shatters into small, blunt pieces, enhancing safety. Polycarbonate is a lightweight, impact-resistant plastic alternative, providing durability and flexibility. Each type serves distinct needs ranging from safety, aesthetics, and performance in vehicle manufacturing and passenger protection.</p></p>
