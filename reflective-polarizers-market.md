<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi Layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflective polarizers are categorized into single-layer and multi-layer types based on their construction and performance characteristics. Single-layer polarizers are simpler and typically more cost-effective, utilizing a single film to achieve polarization, making them suitable for certain applications. In contrast, multi-layer polarizers consist of multiple alternating layers, enhancing performance and efficiency by providing better light transmission and polarization quality. This complexity enables them to be used in advanced applications like displays and optical systems, where superior performance is critical.</p></p>
