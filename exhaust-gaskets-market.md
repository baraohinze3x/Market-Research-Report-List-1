<p><p>The exhaust gaskets market encompasses various types, including exhaust manifold gaskets, exhaust flange gaskets, and header gaskets. Exhaust manifold gaskets create a seal between the engine and the exhaust manifold, preventing leaks and ensuring optimal performance. Exhaust flange gaskets connect different exhaust system components, maintaining pressure and reducing emissions. Header gaskets are used in high-performance applications, sealing headers to the engine for efficient flow. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing engine efficiency and minimizing exhaust leaks in automotive applications.</p></p>
