<p><strong>The Variable Air Volume Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Duct VAV</li><li>Dual Duct VAV</li><li>Induction VAV</li><li>Fan Powered VAV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Variable Air Volume (VAV) systems market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Single Duct VAV** systems regulate airflow through a single duct, adjusting airflow based on zone needs.</p><p>2. **Dual Duct VAV** systems mix hot and cold air from separate ducts to optimize temperature control in different zones.</p><p>3. **Induction VAV** systems use a jet of high-speed air to draw in surrounding air, providing efficient cooling or heating.</p><p>4. **Fan Powered VAV** integrates a small fan to enhance airflow, offering better temperature control and humidity management. Each type caters to specific HVAC demands.</p></p>
