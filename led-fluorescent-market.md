<p><p>The LED fluorescent market comprises several types, each optimizing light output and efficiency. Direct contact LEDs integrate with the phosphor layer for immediate light conversion. Remote phosphor LEDs separate the light source from the phosphor, enhancing thermal performance. Thin film LEDs utilize a slim design for flexible applications. Modular light engines allow customization and scalability for various fixtures. Lastly, package-free designs eliminate traditional enclosures, promoting a compact and efficient approach to lighting solutions. Each type serves unique application needs.</p></p>
