<p><p>The environmentally friendly paper and plastic market encompasses two primary types: dry pressing and wet pressing. Dry pressing involves applying heat and pressure to compress materials, resulting in lightweight, eco-friendly paper substitutes that minimize water usage. In contrast, wet pressing utilizes water to enhance fiber bonding, creating denser and stronger products. Both methods prioritize sustainability by reducing raw material waste and utilizing recycled fibers, catering to growing consumer demand for greener packaging and stationery solutions.</p></p>
