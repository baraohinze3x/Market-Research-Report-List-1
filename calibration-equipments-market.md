<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Calibration Equipments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Calibration Equipments</li><li>Electrical Calibration Equipments</li><li>Physical/Dimensional Calibration Equipments</li><li>Thermodynamic Calibration Equipments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The calibration equipment market is segmented into several types based on the measurement domain. Mechanical calibration equipment includes tools for testing and verifying mechanical properties, such as pressure gauges and torque wrenches. Electrical calibration equipment is focused on electrical parameters, dealing with voltmeters and oscilloscopes. Physical/dimensional calibration equipment measures physical dimensions, utilizing tools like calipers and micrometers. Lastly, thermodynamic calibration equipment encompasses devices for measuring temperature and pressure, including thermocouples and manometers, ensuring instrument accuracy across various industries.</p></p>
