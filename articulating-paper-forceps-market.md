<p><p>The articulating paper forceps market is divided into two main types: straight and curved articulating paper forceps. Straight articulating paper forceps feature parallel jaws that provide precise placement of articulating paper in dental procedures, ensuring consistent bite registrations. In contrast, curved articulating paper forceps have angled jaws that enhance visibility and access to challenging areas within the oral cavity, facilitating effective paper application and patient comfort. Both types are essential tools for dentists to assess occlusal relationships accurately.</p></p>
