<p><p>The rotary valve actuator market comprises several types: pneumatic, hydraulic, electric, and others. Pneumatic actuators utilize compressed air for movement, offering fast response times and simplicity. Hydraulic actuators use fluid pressure for higher force and efficiency in heavy-duty applications. Electric actuators use electric motors for precise control and minimal maintenance, suitable for various settings. Additionally, "others" may include specialized actuators like solenoid or mechanical types, catering to niche applications across diverse industries such as manufacturing, oil and gas, and water treatment.</p></p>
