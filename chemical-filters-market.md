<p><ul><li>Activated Carbon Filters</li><li>Ion-exchange Resin Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical filters market comprises various technologies, notably activated carbon filters and ion-exchange resin filters. Activated carbon filters utilize porous carbon to adsorb impurities, effectively removing volatile organic compounds and chlorine from water and air. In contrast, ion-exchange resin filters swap undesirable ions in a solution with more desirable ones, suitable for softening water and removing heavy metals. Both types are essential in sectors like water treatment, air purification, and industrial processes, ensuring enhanced quality and safety.</p></p>
