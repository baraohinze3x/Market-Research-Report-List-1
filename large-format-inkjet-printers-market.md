<p><p>The large format inkjet printer market offers various ink types tailored for specific applications. Water-based inks are eco-friendly, ideal for indoor graphics. Solvent inks provide durability and weather resistance, suitable for outdoor signage. UV inks cure instantly under UV light, ensuring vibrant colors and longevity, often used for rigid substrates. Latex inks combine the benefits of water-based and solvent inks, resulting in environmentally friendly, versatile prints. Dye sublimation inks deliver high-quality, vivid graphics on textiles and hard surfaces, ideal for promotional items.</p></p>
