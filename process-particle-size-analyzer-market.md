<p><p>The Process Particle Size Analyzer market is divided into two main types: dry and wet. Dry analyzers measure particle size through techniques like laser diffraction or dynamic image analysis, suitable for powders and granules in various industrial applications. Wet analyzers, on the other hand, use liquid dispersions to assess particle size distribution, typically employed in industries like pharmaceuticals and food where samples are often in slurry form. Both types play crucial roles in quality control and product development.</p></p>
