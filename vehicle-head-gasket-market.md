<p><p>The vehicle head gasket market is categorized into three main types: non-metallic, semi-metallic, and metallic. Non-metallic gaskets, typically made from materials like rubber or composite materials, offer good sealing but are less durable at high temperatures. Semi-metallic gaskets combine layers of metal with non-metallic materials, providing a balance of strength and flexibility, making them suitable for various engine conditions. Metallic gaskets, composed entirely of metal, are designed for high-performance applications, offering superior heat resistance and durability.</p></p>
