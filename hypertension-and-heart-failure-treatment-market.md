<p><p>The hypertension and heart failure treatment market encompasses various medication categories, each targeting different mechanisms. Diuretics help eliminate excess fluid, reducing blood pressure and heart workload. Beta blockers decrease heart rate and pressure, improving heart efficiency. ACE inhibitors relax blood vessels, enhancing blood flow. Alpha blockers also relax blood vessels but focus on blocking nerve signals. Other treatments include calcium channel blockers, angiotensin receptor blockers, and vasodilators, diversifying options for managing these cardiovascular conditions effectively.</p></p>
