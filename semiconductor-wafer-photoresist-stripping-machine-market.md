<p><p>The semiconductor wafer photoresist stripping machine market is divided into two primary types: dry process machines and wet process machines. Dry process machines utilize plasma or other gases to remove photoresist layers without using liquids, providing precise control and reduced chemical waste. In contrast, wet process machines employ chemical solutions to dissolve photoresist, offering effective removal capabilities for complex structures. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, catering to different needs and operational efficiencies in the photolithography process.</p></p>
