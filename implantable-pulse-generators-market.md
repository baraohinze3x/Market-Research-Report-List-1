<p><p>The implantable pulse generator (IPG) market is primarily categorized into single lumen and double lumen types. Single lumen IPGs feature a single pathway for electrical stimulation, making them simpler and often more compact, suitable for straightforward applications. In contrast, double lumen IPGs have two pathways, allowing for more complex stimulation and the ability to simultaneously deliver multiple therapies. This versatility caters to a wider range of medical conditions and enhances treatment efficacy, promoting growth in the IPG market.</p></p>
